About

無框架 無國界-陳立喆

Chef Chen is excellent at various kinds of delicate French desserts. He gives his works unique and creative aspects by adding innovative ways of thinking. Hand-made chocolate and chocolate art show piece are especially Chef Chen’s specialties. Art is life to Chef Chen, he would be inspired by scenes, objects or any materials that runs into his life. Anything could be an inspiration to his next creation. Chef Chen is able to decompose things we’re familiar with and reconstruct it with innovative techniques and making pastry art to the most incisive level. Every art piece of Chef Chen broke the fundamental knowledge we had, moreover, it gives perfect balance between art and pastry.
